Cyber Security conference

DEFCON(short for "Defense Readiness Condition") is a series of cybersecurity conferences held annually around the world. These conventions bring together hackers, cybersecurity professionals, government officials, and others interested in information security. At DEFCON, attendees participate in workshops, competitions, and presentations covering a wide range of topics related to hacking, cybersecurity, privacy, and digital rights. It provides a platform for networking, learning, and showcasing cutting-edge research and techniques in the field of cybersecurity. DEFCON is known for its informal atmosphere, emphasis on hands-on learning, and its role as a gathering place for the global hacking community.
